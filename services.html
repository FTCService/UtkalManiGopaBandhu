GOPABANDHU HOME :

Empowering young people with training on care giving for the elderly is a great way to create opportunities for them to serve society while also fulfilling the needs of the elderly population. Such training equips young people with the knowledge and skills to provide compassionate care and support to seniors in hospices, old age homes, and homes where the elderly are staying alone.
